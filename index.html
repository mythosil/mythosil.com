Akito Tabira

Software engineer in Japan.

Love biotechnology, cryptography and natural computing.

I'm working on communication and learning platforms for medical professionals as an engineering manager.

In my previous job I was developing the systems of payment, e-commerce, genomics and etc.

I've been working on a simulator for biochemical reactions since when I was a graduate student. The mathematical models of the reactions are expressed as ODEs in SBML, and our simulator libSBMLSim can solve them.
I'm a member of National Resource for Network Biology (NRNB) organization. We do mentoring for Google Summer of Code. If you're interested in participating in GSoC, see this page and contact us.
