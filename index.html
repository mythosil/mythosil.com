Akito Tabira

Software engineer.
Love biotechnology, cryptography and quantum computing.

I'm a software engineer in Tokyo.

Six years experience in payment services. Their systems were primarily written in Java. Spring Boot fits me really well. I have experience of building Android and iOS apps which can top up and transfer money.

I've been working on genomics systems for over two years. Not only as an software engineer, but also I do research on genome data as a bioinformatician.

Since when I was a graduate student I've been working on simulator for biochemical reactions. The reactions are written in SBML models in the form of ordinary differential equations and our simulator, libSBMLSim, can solves them.

Products

  • libSBMLSim: A library for simulating SBML models.
  • sss4j: Secret sharing scheme library for Java.
  • regzar: Remote control for REGZA TV.
